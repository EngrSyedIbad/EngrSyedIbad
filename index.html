06 — Flagship Work

Research Projects

Deep Learning · CT Imaging

Hybrid CNN-KELM for Renal Lesion Classification

Achieved 99.71% accuracy (multi-class: cysts, stones, tumors) using lightweight CNNs + SMOTE. Clinically deployable AI pipeline for CT-based renal diagnosis.

Agentic AI · LLM

Biomedical Device Troubleshooting Assistant

Safety-constrained agentic LLM with tool-augmented reasoning, policy enforcement, and structured QC guidance for clinical engineering workflows.

Predictive Healthcare

CKD & CVD Risk Prediction (Diabetic Patients)

ML pipeline on Pakistan clinical dataset (n=703). Random Forest achieved 92% accuracy for early-stage kidney and heart disease detection. arXiv:2510.14997.
